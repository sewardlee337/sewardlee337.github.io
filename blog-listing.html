The Young, the Restless, and the Unemployed

December 17, 2014

Tragedy, unfortunately, is strikingly prevalent among young professionals in Taiwan. Simply put, the current level of youth unemployment there is absurd. An article recently published in CommonWealth Magazine points out that youth unemployment in Taiwan surpasses Japan and South Korea, and compares unfavorably with that of OECD countries. Both CommonWealth and my analysis suggest that the tragedy is structurally abnormal.
